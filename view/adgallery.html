Ad Gallery

The network uses the following ad sizes: leaderboard = 675x90 pixels, floating footer = 675x50 pixels, side tower = 200x550 pixels, mobile = 320x50 pixels.

Traditional Image Display Ads

Flat Image

Most common ad format, typically provided by the advertiser or their agency. Accepted: JPG or PNG format image ads. MUST be less than 50 kb.

Animated Gif

Most common animated ad format, typically provided by the advertiser. On average, animated GIF ads receive 15% - 20% more clicks than flat image ads. MUST be less than 50 kb.

Simple Interactive Rich Media Ads

Expansion

Available in all sizes, the expansion ad is the most common rich media ad format and is typically provided by PinPoint. Ad expands to a larger size on mouse-over grabbing attention and engaging the consumer. Typically, these ads enjoy 100% - 150% more clicks than flat image ads. Must be less than 50 kb
