Geo Targeting
Target visitors to PinPoint network sites from any countries or major cities in the world. Ideal for inbound flight specials, regional campaigns, tourism promotions and more.
Age Range Targeting
Target specific age ranges such as 18 – 25 years or 30 – 45 years. Ideal for combination with gender targeting.

Browser & Device Targeting
Target by browser or device such as Firefox, iPad, Chrome, Opera, Safari or IPhone. Ideal for promoting apps, hardware upgrades, software and more.
Niche Targeting
Target your ad to show to specific niche audiences such as restaurant goers, IT professionals, agribusiness, accountants and more.
Mobile Carrier Targeting
Target your ad to show only on mobile devices using a particular telecom provider. Ideal for promoting calling plans, mobile money accounts, mobile internet packages and more.
